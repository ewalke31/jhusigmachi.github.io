Sigma Chi Sweethearts

The girl of my dreams is the sweetest girl, of all the girls I know...

- From the lyrics of "The Sweetheart of Sigma Chi"

Who is a Sweetheart?

Sigma Chis often refer to their wives and girlfriends as "sweethearts," and undergraduates elect official chapter sweethearts each year. The International Fraternity selects one International Sweetheart to serve as an ambassador for a two-year period.

Picture (left to right): Rachel Jandak (14-15), Katie Sacco (13-14), and Kellie Hunn (15-16)

Chapter Sweetheart

Kappa Upsilon's Sweetheart for the 2015-2016 academic year is Kellie Hunn.

Kellie is a sophomore International Studies and Global Environmental Change & Sustainability double major at Johns Hopkins University. She is also secretary of the competitive JHU Equestrian Team and photographer for the JHU News-Letter. Her other hobbies include volleyball, hiking, training horses, and playing fiddle.
