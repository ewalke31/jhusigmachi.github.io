Who is a Sweetheart?

Sigma Chis often refer to their wives and girlfriends as "sweethearts," and undergraduates elect official chapter sweethearts each year. The International Fraternity selects one International Sweetheart to serve as an ambassador for a two-year period.

Chapter Sweethearts

Kappa Upsilon's Sweetheart for the 2017-2018 academic year is Camila Camacho.

Camila is a junior Computer Science major with a minor in Entrepreneurship and Management. She was born in Caracas, Venezuela and grew up in Miami, Florida. She is the vice chair of the Association of Computing Machinery at Hopkins and is also a member of Alpha Phi Omega, the national coed service fraternity. She also works for the IT department at Hopkins on JaySquad, a team of student developers working to improve JHU Student and Administrative Solutions.
